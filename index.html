Job Tips For GEEKS: The Job Search

Official book site

Dave Fecak has just released his first ebook Job Tips For Geeks: The Job Search, which is to be the first in a series designed to help software engineers and other technology professionals to navigate their careers. The book is divided into four chapters which represent the lifecycle of a job search, delivered chronologically.

  • Chapter I – Before the Search includes sections on proactive steps to be taken before starting any activity. Resumes, references, job search strategy, and your online reputation are covered here.

  • Chapter II – Starting the Search provides a guide to implementing your search strategy, the pros and cons of the various search methods (secrets to dealing with agency technical recruiters, job boards, etc.), and a unique tactic I’ve dubbed actively passive search.

  • Chapter III – During the Search talks about activity monitoring and metrics and why you may not be getting positive responses, phone screen and interview preparation, interview tactics, follow-up, and negotiating job offers.

  • Chapter IV – After the Search runs through all the steps after you have chosen which job you want. How to accept and reject offers, write resignation letters, and use your job search to maximize your technology career.
